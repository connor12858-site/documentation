P2PNetwork is built around three service layers that cooperate at runtime.

## Relay Service

- Starts in standalone mode when no bootstrap peers are provided.
- Exposes full node multiaddrs for other peers to connect.
- Tracks connected peers and prints connection events.

## Discovery Service

- Uses a shared discovery topic: `fgov-network`.
- Advertises peers through the DHT routing discovery layer.
- Finds and connects to discovered peers on an interval.

## Peer Connection Service

- Connects by multiaddr with timeout protection.
- Prevents duplicate active connections.
- Supports manual disconnect by peer ID.
